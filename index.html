Pictionary

What is the Pictionay?

A Pictionary is the new way for you to learn about things in a fun and interactive way. It does exactly what a dictionary does, just better, Much BETTER!

Why do we use it?

Have you ever felt bored while learning something? Has finding what things are are no fun to you? Well, we've got you covered. Presenting to you, the Pictionary, the modern day dictionary that lets you find details and information about pictures which makes learning about stuff loads of fun!

Where does it come from?

The pictionary is the brainchild of our team that came up with this incredible idea of turning the process of learning facts a bit more interactive and a lot more fun. A website is just a start, publishing paperbacks and digital copies are the next step in taking this idea forward. Stand with us in our journey and help us make the pictionary initiative a major success.

How to use it?

Explore through different sections of beautiful pictures and give a tickle to your brain. On finding anything interesting, just hover over the picture and enjoy reading about it and find out what you should know about it.
